## ЁЯЫб Task 5 тАУ Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention

**Task 5** is focused on **Cybercrime Awareness**, where I studied a specific cybercrime type relevant to students and created a case study along with a prevention guide.  
The selected crime type can be phishing, UPI/online payment fraud, identity theft, cyberbullying, or ransomware.

For this task:

- I wrote a **200тАУ250 word case study** describing what the cybercrime is, how it usually happens (step-by-step), who is typically targeted, and what the consequences can be.  
- This case study is saved as `casestudy.txt` or `casestudy.pdf` inside the `task-5-cyber crime/` folder.

I also created a **тАЬStay Safe OnlineтАЭ prevention checklist** with at least eight specific and actionable tips designed for college students in India, including:

- Safe behaviour for social media and messaging  
- Extra care with links, attachments, and unknown contacts  
- At least two tips focused on **UPI/online payment safety**  

The checklist clearly mentions the official reporting channels:

- **National Cyber Crime Portal**: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- **Cyber Crime Helpline**: 1930  

This checklist is stored as `stay-safe-online.txt` and can be used as a ready-made awareness resource.
